Che Pizza
Un po’di paradiso italiano
(A little bit of italian heaven)

Che pizza! A little italian to spice up the day. In 1999, a family started to make pizzas from their house and slowly but surely it grew to be big enough to open up their own store. The founders migrated from Italy and had started a business here in the U.S. Since then, it has grown internationally.

Our company is not just any normal pizzeria company but one that wants you to be essere pieno come un uovo (To be really full and stuffed so that you can’t eat anything else). Around here, we don’t settle for anything less than food we’re proud to serve. Our goal is to make people happy.
